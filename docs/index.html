What is LABRACADABRA??!?

  • a HACKATHON where current students and lab friends hack together to re-visit past Media Lab projects using new technologies and fresh perspectives.
  • hands-on WORKSHOPS (a.k.a TIME CAPSULES) by distinguished alumni from various eras:
  • an EXHIBITION showcasing historic Media Lab pieces and untold stories that make up the lab. Do you have artefacts to contribute? Email us!
  • the first-of-its-kind LABARET (think Cabaret but it's the Media Lab... :) where the results of the hackathon and the workshops will be performed and live-demoed. Perform or perish!
  • the legendary 99Fridays PARTY to cap off the week!
