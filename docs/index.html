Attention!! Media Labbers of Old and New!

In preparation for the upcoming 40th anniversary of the MIT Media Lab (Oct 2025), prepare yourselves to be dazzled and bewildered by the most spectacular show this side of reality!

Welcome to...

LABRACADABRA

The Untold Histories of the Future

15-18 Oct 2024

We invite you to co-create in this week-long future-history festival where students, alumni, staff, and faculty will dive into four decades of impactful, playful, and quirky past to re-imagine our vision for the future.

What is LABRACADABRA??!?

  • a HACKATHON where current students and lab friends hack together to re-visit past Media Lab projects using new technologies and fresh perspectives. The kick-off opening ceremony starts at Oct 15 6pm..!
  • hands-on WORKSHOPS (a.k.a TIME CAPSULES) by distinguished alumni from various eras:
    • Oct 16th 10am: Media Lab Confessionals: Generative Critique (by Alexis Hope MAS21' & ???)
    • Oct 16th 6pm: The Machine Cabaret (by Kelly Dobson MAS02' & Sophia Brueckner MAS14')
    • Oct 17 10am: The Cardboard Research Institute (by Che-Wei Wang MAS15' & Jonathan Bobrow MAS16')
    • Oct 17 6pm: Social Fabrics Re-imagined: From Attention Economies to Intention Ecologies (by Ziv Epstein MAS23', Judith Donath MAS96', Rubez Chong MAS20')
  • an EXHIBITION (Oct 18th 6pm) showcasing historic Media Lab work as well as shining light on the untold stories that make up the lab. Do you have artefacts to contribute? Email us!
  • and of course, the legendary 99Fridays PARTY (Oct 18th 9pm) to cap off the week!
