October 15-18, 2024

What is LABRACADABRA??!?

  • a HACKATHON where current students and lab friends hack together to re-visit past Media Lab projects using new technologies and fresh perspectives.
  • hands-on WORKSHOPS (a.k.a TIME CAPSULES) by distinguished alumni from various eras. Check out the awesome schedule ↓↓↓
    • The Machine Cabaret by Kelly Dobson MAS02', Sophia Brueckner MAS14', and Rébecca Kleinberger MAS20'
    • Social Fabrics Re-imagined: From Attention Economies to Intention Ecologies by Ziv Epstein MAS23', Judith Donath MAS96', Rubez Chong MAS20'
    • The Cardboard Research Institute by Che-Wei Wang MAS15', Jonathan Bobrow MAS16', and Taylor Levy MAS15'
    • Media Lab Confessionals: Behind the Curtain by Alexis Hope MAS21' & Cassie Lee MAS24'
  • an EXHIBITION showcasing historic Media Lab projects and untold stories that make up the lab. Got an old gizmo or a curious contraption to contribute? Email us!
  • the first-of-its-kind LABARET (think Cabaret but it's the Media Lab... :) where the magical fruits of the hackathon and workshops will be demo-ed live on stage. Perform or perish!
  • the legendary 99Fridays PARTY to cap off the week!

Do you have an idea you're excited to co-create? Looking for fellow hackers/builders? Please contribute to our Brainstorming Board


Labracadabra is conceptualized and organized by ML students with hope to bring the ML alumni community closer together, backed by the support and insights from the E14 Fund.
